Privacy Policy

Nothing leaves your device

Last updated July 2026

The short version: every letter, photo, and voice memo you seal into a capsule is stored only in this app's local storage on your iPhone. Openlater has no server, no account system, no analytics, and no network connection of any kind. We could not read your capsules even if we wanted to.

What Openlater stores, and where

Text letters are stored in a local database inside the app's private storage area. Photos and voice-memo recordings are saved as ordinary files (JPEG and M4A) inside the app's own Application Support folder. All of it stays on the device where you created it.

What Openlater does not do

  • No iCloud or CloudKit sync of any kind.
  • No account, sign-in, or email address is ever requested.
  • No data is uploaded to any server — Openlater makes no network requests at all.
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs are included.
  • No third party, including the developer, can access the contents of your capsules.

Camera roll and microphone access

If you choose to attach a photo, Openlater asks the Photos app to hand you a picture you pick — the picture is copied into the app's own local storage and Openlater never browses your library on its own. If you record a voice memo, the recording is written straight to a local file and is only ever played back by you, on this device.

Deleting your data

Deleting a capsule inside the app permanently deletes its file from the device immediately. Deleting the app itself removes everything Openlater ever stored, with nothing left behind anywhere else.

Subscriptions

Openlater Pro is an auto-renewable subscription processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Openlater never sees or stores your payment information — that relationship is between you and Apple.

Children

Openlater is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, of any age, because it does not collect information at all.
