The app

Openlater lets you write, photograph, or record a letter and lock it behind a future date you choose. All content stays on your device — see the Privacy Policy for the full detail.

Free tier and subscription

Openlater is free to use for up to three sealed, text-only capsules. Openlater Pro is an auto-renewable monthly subscription, billed through your Apple ID, that unlocks unlimited capsules, photo and voice-memo capsules, and recurring capsules. Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period, and can be managed or cancelled at any time in your device's App Store account settings.

Your content

You own everything you write, photograph, or record inside Openlater. Because nothing is transmitted anywhere, you are solely responsible for backing up anything you would be upset to lose if your device is lost, damaged, or reset — Openlater cannot recover content that was never uploaded anywhere.

The seal is permanent, so use it thoughtfully

A capsule's content is genuinely inaccessible before its unlock date — there is no support request, password reset, or developer override that can open a capsule early. Please choose your unlock dates carefully.
