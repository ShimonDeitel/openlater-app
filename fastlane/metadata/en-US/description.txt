Openlater is a time capsule for your own words. Write a letter, pick a future "open when..." date - a birthday, an anniversary, or any day worth waiting for - and seal it shut with a literal wax seal.

The lock is real. Openlater checks every capsule's unlock date against today whenever you open the app, so a capsule stays sealed for every moment before its date and becomes readable the instant that day arrives - there is no way to peek early, not even for you. When the day comes, watch the wax seal actually crack and break open before your letter is revealed.

The free tier holds up to 3 sealed capsules, text only. Openlater Pro is an auto-renewing monthly subscription that unlocks unlimited capsules, photo capsules, voice-memo capsules you record yourself, and recurring capsules - seal a birthday letter once and it re-seals itself automatically for next year, every year.

Everything lives only on your device. Letters, photos, and voice recordings are stored locally and are never uploaded, synced, or sent anywhere - there is no account, no server, and no internet connection required.

A keepsake for the letters you want to send to the future.
